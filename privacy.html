Last Updated: July 2026

R@THR UN1Q ("the App") respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it. The App may automatically collect the following non-personal data:

  • Basic usage data (such as app opens, feature usage)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Anonymous analytics data
  • Crash logs used to diagnose issues

The App does not collect health data, financial data, biometric identifiers, or any sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Information

Any collected information is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Maintain app stability and security

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services to improve functionality, including:

  • Microsoft App Center (analytics and crash reporting)

These services may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard security practices. We do not store personal identifiers, and we do not maintain user profiles.
